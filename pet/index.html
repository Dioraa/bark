🐶 Dog Care Tips

  • 🦴 Always provide clean water and a balanced diet.
  • 🐾 Regular walks and playtime keep your dog happy and healthy.
  • 💉 Schedule vet visits at least once a year.
  • 🛁 Bathe your dog every 4–6 weeks or as needed.
  • 🦷 Brush their teeth regularly to avoid dental issues.
  • 🎾 Mental stimulation like training or toys is just as important as physical exercise.
  • 📋 Make sure your dog wears an ID tag and is microchipped in case they get lost.

About Us

At Cedar Ridge Pet Care, we are more than just a service — we are a home away from home for your furry friends. Our passionate and experienced team is dedicated to providing top-quality grooming, safe and cozy boarding, and professional veterinary care with genuine love and attention.

We understand that pets are part of the family, and we treat each one as if they were our own. Whether your pet needs a refreshing spa day, a fun and secure place to stay while you’re away, or a caring health check-up, we are here to ensure they receive the best possible care.

With clean facilities, outdoor play areas, and a commitment to safety and comfort, Cedar Ridge is where tails wag and hearts feel at ease. Your pet’s happiness is our priority — because at Cedar Ridge, your pets are family.
